Hello there!

To get your temporary email address type in your email to the left and press save, then right click in a field (on a normal webpage, not on this options page).

A temporary email address is active only as logn as your email generation settings allows it to. So it will either become inactive after you've received a number of emails or become inactive after the maximum amount of time it's allowed to exist has been exeeded.

Everytime you ask for a temporary email address a new one will be provided for you.

Created by Kit Sunde

I created this little extension after watching a talk by Philip Hofstetter where he outlined how he built and deployed a node.js application called tempalias.com (the service the plugin is using). The talk was given at JSConf.eu in 2010.
